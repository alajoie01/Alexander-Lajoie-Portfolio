Bob & Betty Beyster Lab VR Recreation

This project recreates a laboratory of the University of Michigan Betty & Beyster Building with numerous interactable objects and actors. This project shows competency in Unreal Engine (version 5.1 was used for this project) as well as its subsystems and functions.

This project features:
• Interactable light switches
• Togglable fire alarms
• Working trash cans
• Working whiteboards
• Interactable non-playable characters
• Animated background characters
• High-quality textures and models
• Simple and intuitive interaction system
And much more!
